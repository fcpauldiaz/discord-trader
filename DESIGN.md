# Trade Platform — Design Register

## Brand
- Name: Trade Platform
- Tone: Calm, precise, trustworthy (not hype trading bro)
- Palette: Sea/lagoon OKLCH tokens from starter (`--sea-ink`, `--lagoon-deep`, `--chip-bg`, `--line`)

## Typography
- Display: bold tracking-tight for hero and page titles
- Body: 14–16px, soft ink for secondary copy

## Components
- `island-shell`: elevated cards with subtle border
- `nav-link`: header navigation with active state
- Dashboard cells: green/red OKLCH intensity by P&L magnitude

## Dashboard patterns
- 7-column calendar grid with weekday headers
- KPI strip: 4 equal cards on `sm+`
- Trade table: sticky header row, horizontal scroll on mobile

## Accessibility
- Sufficient contrast on calendar cells (text on tinted background)
- Form labels associated with inputs on settings/connections

## Legal
- Footer/disclaimer: not financial advice; trading involves risk
